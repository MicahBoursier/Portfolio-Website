Inktober 2018

October 2018 | Art Project

In October of 2018, I committed to the inktober challenge. Inktober is a challenge created by artists on the internet, in which you are tasked with inking a drawing for ever day of october. There are official prompts for each day on the inktober website, but instead, I used prompts provided by my art class. some days, I even combined prompts to make it more challenging.

By the end of october, I had produced 31 drawings, and, looking back, my skills with ink had significantly increased. Before Inktober, I hadn't so much as touched the medium, but by the end, I felt I was fairly good at controlling my line weight, and tapering, etc. If nothing else, I made a fun collection of interesting, creative ideas.
